THE VISUAL LANGUAGE

Since the beginning of humanity, the expressions and feelings are made by symbols where we can verbalize or visualize the thoughts. In the visual language, we can communicate using elements such as colors, texture, format, symbols, sizes, etc. Each element can have its own meaning or effect in our brain, the colors, for example, as a warm color, or a cold one, light, dark, etc. There are many studies about it, where we can learn how to communicate better in a visual language. Everyday more people from all around the world are connected spending more time online. The visual language is really important to keep the people connected, where the right design, letters, symbols and colors can show faster in a visual language the proposal of the content.

Design and Visual Language

The human is the only being in earth that can comunicate using different visual languages and symbols.

The study of typography is really important, because the letters can have different sizes, thicknesses, and forms, that can express one same word in different ways.
